Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection

At TextToolPro, we do not collect personal identification information. Most of our tools are client-side, meaning the text you input is processed locally on your computer and is not sent to our servers.

2. Log Files

We follow a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. This is part of hosting services' analytics. The information collected includes IP addresses, browser type, and date/time stamps.

3. Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of the third-party vendors on our site. It uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet.

4. Security

We value your trust in providing us your information. We strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
