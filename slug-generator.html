What is a URL Slug?

A slug is the part of a URL that identifies a page in a human-readable format. It’s vital for SEO as search engines use it to determine the content of your page.

Why use Slugs?

Clean, hyphenated slugs are much easier for users to share and for Google to index compared to URLs with random numbers or characters.
